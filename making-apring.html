Anti-procrastination Ring, 2023





Zihan Zhou
Anti-procrastination Ring, 2023
Rings
Dimensions: range from 16-20mm (inner diameter).
Material: resin, red thread.



There are so many things I want to do, but I always procrastinate.
I need a physical object to remind me not to procrastinate,
Something you can wear and touch all the time: a ring, for example.
Wear this ring and remind yourself every time you feel lazy,
Until the power of action is etched in your consciousness,
Until you no longer need this ring.
Stop procrastinating, and happy working!
